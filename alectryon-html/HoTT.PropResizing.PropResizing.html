Axiom resize_hprop : forall `{PropResizing} (A : Type@{i}) `{IsHProp A}, Type@{j}.
Axiom equiv_resize_hprop : forall `{PropResizing} (A : Type@{i}) `{IsHProp A},

       `{PropResizing} (A : Type) `{IsHProp A}
